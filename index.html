Welcome to Math Assistance

We aim to provide comprehensive mathematical assistance for visually impaired individuals through a web application. Users can perform various mathematical calculations, ranging from basic arithmetic to complex problem-solving, with ease. The application supports voice input and output, allowing users to interact using voice commands and receive responses through speech synthesis. Additionally, the integration with MATLAB engine enhances the capabilities for tackling advanced mathematical challenges. With a user-friendly interface and accessibility features, our project empowers blind users to overcome mathematical barriers effectively.
